Getting Started with Garage Band

Garage Band is the most advanced WordPress theme for bands the world has ever seen.

Skip to Installation Instructions

How to Install

Install Garage Band Through WordPress:

  1. Log into WordPress through the admin panel. (i.e. www.yourdomain.com/wp-admin)
  2. Go to the "Appearance" module in the left navigation and select "Themes"
  3. You should see two tabs "Manage Themes" and "Install Themes", Click on "Install Themes".
  4. Now click on "Upload" in the sub nav.
  5. Select the Garage Band ZIP file located on your local machine.
  6. Click Install.
  7. After your theme installs you should get a confirmation that it was successful, you will have the option to "Activate", "Preview", or "Return to Themes Page".
  8. Click "Activate".
  9. Success! Your new UpThemes Garage Band Theme is now active.

Install Garage Band Manually:

  1. Upload the Garage Band theme files to the themes folder located on your server. (i.e. wp-content/themes/)
  2. Once this is completed log into WordPress through the admin panel. (i.e. www.yourdomain.com/wp-admin)
  3. Go to the "Appearance" module in the left navigation and select "Themes".
  4. You should see two tabs "Manage Themes" and "Install Themes" click on "Manage Themes".
  5. In this tab you will see "Current Theme" which is the standard WordPress theme or perhaps another theme you had installed previously. Also,"Available Themes" which are the list of themes that you have uploaded to your server. You should see Garage Band 1.0 in the available themes section. Click "Activate".
  6. Success! Your new UpThemes Garage Band Theme is now active.

Updating the Featured Image Area

Uploading Featured Images:

  1. Click on the "Feature Images" menu item in the left navigation area.
  2. Upload a new image by choosing an image from your local machine.
  3. Click upload.
  4. Your image will be uloaded and displayed below the upload field.
  5. You can now add a link to that image if you'd like it to link to a specific URL.
  6. Click Update for that URL setting to take effect.

Editing the Featured Image Slider Settings

  1. You can now update the settings of the Featured Image Settings area to whatever suits your needs.
  2. Check if you'd like to enable the transition effects, if left uncheck the images will not transition and it will stay on the first image in the list
  3. Select your desired trasition effect. Whatever you select here will show that style of transition.
  4. Set your transition delay. This is the length of time it will take to start the transition to another image. The longer the time, the longer the image will be displayed to the user.
  5. Set your transition length. This is the length of time it takes to transition to the next image. The longer the time the longer the transition effect will take to completely expose the next image.
  6. Save Settings and your settings should take effect.

Adding Albums and Managing Music

Adding an Album

  1. Click on the "Albums" menu item in the left navigation area, or click the menu arrow to expose the "Add New" link.
  2. You can click on the "Add New" (Either on the menu item or on the button next to the Albums header).
  3. Give your album a title in the title field.
  4. Set the album image by clicking on "Set Feature Image" link.
  5. Once you click "Set Feature Image" a WordPress upload modal window will come up. You can either upload an image from your local machine by clicking select files, or from a url the the media library. Lets upload a new file so click "Select Files".
  6. A system dialog box will come up, select the file from your local machine and click ok.
  7. Your file will upload and you will see your image displayed. Go ahead and click "Use as featured image" below. Don't worry about the size as the album will be automatically resized and cropped.
  8. Now click "Save all changes".
  9. Close the modal window and you should see your album image in the feature images module area.
  10. Enter a description in the text area field.
  11. Enter the album price
  12. Add your iTunes link (If available).
  13. Add your Amazon link (If available).
  14. Add your custom link (If available).
  15. Add the release date.
  16. Now you can add mp3's for people to stream while on your homepage of your site. First you need to upload your mp3's by clicking on the "upload" audio icon at the top of the description text area field. This will bring up the WordPress upload modal window.
  17. Select a file from your local machine and save all changes.
  18. Close the modal window. Now you have your MP3's attached to this album post.
  19. Now enter a track name in the "Track Name" field in the "Album Track Information" Module.
  20. Click the upload icon in the "Edit" column.
  21. A modal window will pop up with the list of Mp3's available to attach to this particular track name. Select a track and click "assign audio to track".
  22. You can now add the tracks iTunes, Amazon and Custom Links for people to download your music.
  23. Click save tracks and your set.
  24. Click publish and your album will be added to the site.

Adding Shows, Tours, Venues and Locations

Adding a show on a tour with a venue and a location:

  1. Click on the "Shows" menu item in the left navigation area, or click the menu arrow to expose all of the shows options.
  2. Click "Add New".
  3. Enter a Title for the show in the "Title Field".
  4. Add a description of the show in the "Text Area Field" .
  5. Add the showtime by clicking on the "Showtime" field, a date and time picker will be displayed.
  6. Add a ticket URL in the "Tickey URL" field.
  7. Add admission info like cover charge or if the show is all ages, by clicking on the "Admission Info" field.
  8. To make your show apart of a tour you can do so by adding a tour by click on the "Add New Tour" link or select a tour from a previous tour created.
  9. Venues act the same as Tours, you can add one by clicking "Add New Venue" or select a venue from a previous venue created in the list.
  10. Locations act the same as Tours and Venues, you can add one by clicking "Add New Location" or select a location from a previous locations created in the list.
  11. Click publish, and your show will be added to the list of shows.

Setting up Fan-Generated Photo Gallery

  1. Garage Band has a great way for allowing your fans to submit their photos to your website. But first you need to setup the page to do this.
  2. First you need to setup a "Collection". To do this you need to go over to the "Fan Galleries" menu item in the left navigation area.
  3. Click on the "Collections" link.
  4. When you get to the collections page you need to create a new collection by entering the name and that's it. All the other fields are optional. Once you have given the collection a name, click "add new collection".
  5. You will then see your collection added to the list to the right.
  6. Now we need to link to that collection so that users can submit their photos. To do this we need to go to the "Garage Band" menu item in the left navigation area. Click on this menu item's drop down arrow and click the "Fan Gallery Submission Form".
  7. Now you will need to scroll to the option "Select Collections to Allow" and select the collections you want to allow. You may allow multiple collections for users to submit to by just COMMAND (CTRL on a PC) + Left mouse clicking on them.
  8. Once you've done that go ahead and click "Save Changes"
  9. Now we need to create the submission form. To do that you need to go to the "Pages" menu item and click "Add New".
  10. Now give your page a title and in the right module area of the add new post page you will see a "Page Attributes" modul. In that module there is a drop down labeled "Template" click that drop down and select the "Fan Gallery Submission Page".
  11. Now publish that page.
  12. We're almost done. Now you need to add that page to the main menu. To do that click on the "Appearance" menu item in the left navigation area. Then click on "Menus".
  13. Add the page to your menu, and your done.
  14. Try it out by visiting that page and submitting a photo. You will be able to see the results by going to the collection page.

Setting Up Multiple Blogs

  1. To setup a blog for each member of the band it's easy as creating a category for each member. To do this just go to the "Posts" menu item and click the drop down arrow and click "Categories"
  2. Add a new category for your band member by entering the name and clicking "Add New Category"
  3. Now click on the "Settings" menu item in the left navigation area
  4. You need to make sure your Permalinks are setup go ahead and click on "Permalinks" to do so.
  5. Once that is done create a link within one of your pages to your new category blog.
  6. Just make sure to select the category when posting to your blog.

Setting up Band Members Page

  1. Add the band members as administrators, editors, authors, or contributors on your wordpress site by going to Users > Add New.
  2. Fill in their profile information.
  3. There will be a checkbox at the bottom of that page that you will want to check to enable them as a band member and a field for a subheading. You can enter their instrument played or role in the band. It will show up under their name on the Band Member page.
  4. To create the Band Members page, simple go to Pages > New and create a new page.
  5. On the right side, there will be a drop-down for Page Template. Select the Band Members page template and you should start to see any band members you've added on that page once you've hit Preview.
